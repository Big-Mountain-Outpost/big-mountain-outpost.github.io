What is DateDots?

Most alarm apps think in weekdays. DateDots thinks in dates. Build routines — groups of alarms that belong together — and schedule them onto the exact calendar days you need them. Every scheduled day shows a color-coded dot, so one glance at the month tells you what's coming.

  • Calendar-first scheduling — assign alarm routines to exact dates, not just "every Tuesday".
  • Routines — group a sequence of alarms and toggle or schedule the whole set at once.
  • Timeline alarms — set named alarms before and after an event time, save the timeline as a template, and reuse it on any date.
  • Solo alarms — classic one-time, daily, and weekday alarms.
  • Schedule import — bring dates in from a spreadsheet or your phone's calendar, processed entirely on your device.
  • Built to ring — full-screen alarms on the lock screen, a persistent banner while using other apps, and pre-alarm notifications you can dismiss with one tap.
  • Optional backup & sync — sign in with Google to keep your alarms synced across devices, or stay fully on-device.
